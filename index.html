Headspace · Tilt Check

How are you feeling right now?

Duke: your emotional state is a direct input to decision quality. Anxiety, pressure, and depletion bias toward loss aversion and short-termism. Euphoria biases toward overconfidence. Be honest.

⚠️ Tilt warning. You may be on tilt. Duke's core advice: if you can wait, wait. If you can't, explicitly name what emotion is pulling at your reasoning before you proceed.

Headspace · Now

How are you feeling as you review this?

Pride and relief distort upward. Regret and frustration distort downward. Duke: separate "I feel bad about the outcome" from "it was a bad decision."

⚠️ Resulting risk. Strong emotions during review can cause you to misread whether the process was sound. Try to hold the question: "Given what I knew then, was this a reasonable bet?"
